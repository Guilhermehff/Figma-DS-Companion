## Review Notes

- Burgundy lands at `700` because the supplied swatch is already a dark, high-contrast brand red rather than a middle accent.
- Copper lands at `400` because the source color behaves as a bright accent rather than a deep tone.
- Northstar's off-white, gray, and near-black are treated as one branded neutral ladder so the semantic neutral system can stay coherent without duplicating isolated one-off swatches.
- No third expressive semantic lane is promoted; any extra burgundy reuse stays global-only so the semantic brand system remains two-lane.
