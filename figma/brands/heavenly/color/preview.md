## Review Notes

- The proposed Heavenly palette reuses exact universal white and black, then stages three branded families for the remaining signature accent colors.
- Red is staged as the primary brand ladder because it dominates the supplied palette and typography examples, while the two blues form secondary and tertiary support lanes.
- No branded neutral ladder is proposed because the supplied neutral values are exact universal matches.

## Recommended Semantic Mapping

- `brand/*` -> `heavenly/snow_flower_red/*`
- `brand_secondary/*` -> `heavenly/deep_water_blue/*`
- `neutral/*` remains inherited from the base semantic collection
- Global-only extra families: `heavenly/shore_blue/*`
