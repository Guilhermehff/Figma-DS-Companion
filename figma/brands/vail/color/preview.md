## Review Notes

- The five Vail families land on different source anchor steps because the official source colors occupy different lightness zones in OKLCH.
- Vintage Blue anchors at `600` and Dark Blue anchors at `500`, preserving the primary brand palette without replacing the digital accent families that already drive semantic mapping.
- Digital Blue, Navy, Yellow, Vintage Blue, and Dark Blue all produce 900 and 950 steps that support white text at or above the required threshold.
- The first intake shows that source-anchor selection cannot stay centered on 500 for every family without distorting official brand colors.
- Vail Gray is intentionally mapped to the universal neutral system rather than expanded into a brand family.
